Frustum Calculator

This application will help you create a tapered conical section of any dimension.
Specify the desired diameter at each end of the part and the sheet metal patern will be generated for your convenience.

Frustum Definition:
The portion of a cone or pyramid that remains after its upper section has been cut off by a plane parallel to its base, or that is intercepted between two such planes.(Sometimes mispelled "frustrum".)
